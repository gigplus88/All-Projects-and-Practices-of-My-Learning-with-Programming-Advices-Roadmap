 <h2>Method 1: Center with <code>margin: auto</code></h2>
   <p>
      Convert the `< img >` to block and give it left/right auto margins.
      The image will center itself within its container.
    </p>
    <p> Used When: <br> <ul>
        <li>Quick, minimal markup</li>
        <li>Works in all browsers (IE9+)</li>
        <li>No extra containers required</li>
    </ul>
